received none. Scheduled machine runs continuing during a pre-empted
week are NOT a violation.

before the offer-2 gate (BLUEPRINT §0). Adding any = register-class.

(BLUEPRINT §7). Frequency drops when spend trends high; caps never rise.

infrastructure against own repos"; no production/availability/autonomy
claims, ever.
